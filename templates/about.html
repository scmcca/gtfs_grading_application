About Sequoia

Standardizing and Improving GTFS Data

The General Transit Feed Specification (GTFS) allows transit agencies to describe their schedule operations for automated consumption by trip planning applications.

When the schedule described in GTFS matches the schedule published in other formats, such as agency websites, printed PDFs, and physical signage, riders are able to confidently plan and execute their journeys. When these formats disagree, riders can become confused and lose confidence in transit.

This tool allows anyone to consistently grade the consistency of an agency’s GTFS feed with other sources according to the methodology described in MobilityData’s GTFS Grading Scheme.

About
